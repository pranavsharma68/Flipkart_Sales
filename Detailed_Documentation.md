# Flipkart Sales Analysis – Full Case Study

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes Flipkart sales data to uncover key insights related to product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and revenue trends. The objective was to transform raw e-commerce data into meaningful insights and build an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The business lacked clear visibility into:

- Which products and categories generate the highest revenue
- Customer purchasing patterns and preferences
- Pricing and discount impact on sales performance
- Trends in sales over time

The goal was to identify growth opportunities, optimize product strategy, and improve overall sales performance.

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset includes e-commerce transactional data such as:

- Product details (Product Name, Category)
- Pricing information (Original Price, Discounted Price)
- Customer interactions (Ratings, Reviews)
- Sales-related metrics (Units sold, Revenue)
- Additional attributes (Discount %, Product popularity indicators)

The dataset required cleaning and transformation before analysis.

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

### In Excel:
- Removed duplicates and handled missing values
- Standardized product categories and text fields
- Converted pricing columns into consistent formats
- Created calculated columns (e.g., discount %, revenue metrics)

### Data Transformation:
- Structured dataset for analysis and dashboarding
- Ensured consistency across numerical and categorical fields

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Power BI → Dashboard creation and visualization
- Analytical Techniques → Trend analysis, segmentation, comparative analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights (With Metrics)
- A small percentage of products contribute to a major share (~40–50%) of total revenue
- Products with higher discounts show increased sales volume but reduced profit margins
- Certain categories dominate overall sales, contributing over 60% of total revenue
- Highly rated products (4★ and above) generate significantly higher sales
- Sales trends show periodic spikes, indicating seasonal demand patterns

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on top-performing products to maximize revenue
- Optimize discount strategies to balance sales growth and profitability
- Improve product quality and customer experience to increase ratings
- Invest in high-performing categories
- Use customer feedback to guide product improvements

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview (Power BI)
The interactive dashboard includes:

- Total Sales and Revenue KPIs
- Product and category performance analysis
- Discount vs Sales relationship
- Customer rating impact analysis
- Time-based sales trends

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly explore performance and identify business opportunities.
